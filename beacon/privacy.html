Beacon Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

What we collect

Beacon collects your name, phone number, and other information you provide when using the app. We also store your activity within the app.

How we use it

We use your information to provide the Beacon service. We use your phone number to send SMS notifications as part of the app's functionality. We do not sell your data to third parties.

SMS messaging

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages from Beacon related to your use of the service. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Data sharing

Certain information may be shared with other users as part of normal app functionality. Your phone number is never shared with other users.

Data retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.
