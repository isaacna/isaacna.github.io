Beacon Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

What we collect

Beacon collects your name, phone number or email address, and other information you provide when using the app. We also store your activity within the app.

How we use it

We use your information to provide the Beacon service. We use your phone number to send SMS notifications as part of the app's functionality. We do not sell your data to third parties.

SMS messaging

To create a Beacon account, you must provide your phone number or email address and explicitly accept these terms, which include consent to receive SMS messages related to app functionality. After submitting your phone number, you will receive a one-time passcode (OTP) via SMS to verify your number and complete registration. Completing this step confirms your phone number and constitutes your opt-in to receive SMS messages from Beacon.

Only users who have completed this registration flow will receive SMS messages from Beacon. All messages include opt-out instructions. You can reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages. Message and data rates may apply.

Data sharing

Certain information may be shared with other users as part of normal app functionality. Your phone number is never shared with other users.

Data retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.
